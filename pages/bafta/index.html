BAFTA Television Craft Awards  ·  2024

Awards Judge

Category  /  Titles & Graphic Identity

Served as a judge for the BAFTA Television Craft Awards 2024 in the Titles & Graphic Identity category — evaluating outstanding creative work in motion design, title sequences, and graphic identity across British television productions.

As a judge, responsibilities included reviewing submissions from leading studios and independent creatives, assessing craft, innovation, and impact in title design and on-screen graphic identity within the competitive landscape of contemporary television.

Membership

Eri Adachi is a Full Member of BAFTA — the British Academy of Film and Television Arts — one of the world's most prestigious organisations dedicated to supporting, developing, and promoting the art of film, television, and games.

BAFTA Full Membership is open to practitioners with a significant body of professional work in their field. As a Full Member, Eri actively participates in the Academy's activities, including voting for BAFTA Awards and contributing to industry initiatives that champion excellence and diversity in visual storytelling.

This long-standing commitment to the Academy reflects a dedication not only to the craft of compositing, but to the broader creative community that shapes the future of film and television.
