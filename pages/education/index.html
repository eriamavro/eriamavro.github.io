Education
& Outreach

Initiative

Independent UK–Japan education and outreach initiatives, supporting engagement with programmes such as BAFTA — particularly in youth and student-focused contexts.

These initiatives aim to build bridges between the British and Japanese creative industries, fostering awareness of world-class film and television institutions among emerging practitioners and students.

Vision

With over 20 years in the visual effects industry, I continue to work in post-production while also sharing practical experience with the next generation. I work with Japanese students in the UK, helping to connect them with the British creative industries and offering insight into both environments, with a longer-term aim of extending this to creatives in Japan.
