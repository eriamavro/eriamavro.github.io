Education
& Outreach

Initiative

Independent UK–Japan education and outreach initiatives, supporting engagement with programmes such as BAFTA — particularly in youth and student-focused contexts.

These initiatives aim to build bridges between the British and Japanese creative industries, fostering awareness of world-class film and television institutions among emerging practitioners and students.

Vision

With over 20 years in the visual effects industry, the goal is to share that experience meaningfully — not only through the work itself, but by opening doors for the next generation of visual storytellers.

Particular focus is placed on connecting young people at Japanese schools in the UK with opportunities and institutions in the UK creative industries, drawing on first-hand knowledge of both professional environments — with a longer-term vision of extending this reach to young creatives in Japan itself.
